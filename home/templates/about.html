The Dutchtreats story

As a Dutchman who has lived abroad for several years, I love to try local food and enjoy all cuisines. However, wherever I went there were typical Dutch snacks and treats that I missed. Whenever I speak to someone who has spend some time in The Netherlands, they all say the same thing. Dutch treats are the best! This inspired me to create Dutchtreats. I thought there was a market for shipping typical Dutch foods to people all over the world.

We started small, shipping within Europe. But we grew rapidly and now we ship to four continents and have distribution centers in 10 countries. Our team consists of experts in logistics and we make sure all products are delivered promptly and fresh. Please have a look at our wide range of products and feel free to contact us with any comments, questions, or suggestions.
